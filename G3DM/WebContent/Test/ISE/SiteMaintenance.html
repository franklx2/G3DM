Email Us

You can email us at studentaid@ed.gov anytime. We’ll respond within about a day, except for federal holidays.

Top

Chat With Us

You can access private online chat Monday through Friday between 8 a.m. and 11 p.m. Eastern time (ET), and on Saturdays and Sundays between 11 a.m. and 5 p.m. ET. (Chat is not available on some federal holidays, the day after Thanksgiving, and Dec. 24.)

Top

Call Us

You can call us at 1-800-4-FED-AID (1-800-433-3243) or 334-523-2691. If you are hearing impaired, call the TTY line at 1-800-730-8913.

You can call us Monday through Friday between 8 a.m. and 11 p.m. Eastern time (ET), and on Saturdays and Sundays between 11 a.m. and 5 p.m. ET. (The call center is closed on some federal holidays, the day after Thanksgiving, and Dec. 24.) You can use our automated telephone services to get recorded information and conduct some types of business 24 hours a day.

Get Help With fafsa.gov

While you’re filling out your FAFSA, you can click on the Help icon at the top of the screen and follow the links to a secure online chat session where you can ask our customer service representatives a question.

Top

Get Help With Your Student Loan

Trying to make a payment on your federal student loan? You need to pay your loan servicer (the organization managing your loan repayment). If you’re not sure who services your loan(s), visit the National Student Loan Data System to find out.
